Our Privacy Policy

Newsletter

We collect emails and send newsletters through MailerLite. Our newsletters all have tracking disabled through MailerLite's services. You can read their full privacy policy here: https://www.mailerlite.com/legal/privacy-policy

Tickets at events

When selling tickets for events, we use Ticketbutler. If you opt in to our newsletter while buying tickets at TicketButler, we may export your email address and name to the MailerLite website. You can read their full privacy policy here: https://ticketbutler.io/en/privacy-policy/

Individual rights

We respect all GDPR rights. If you want to know if we have your contacts or be deleted, please email info@django-denmark.org. We can only respond to inquiries about the sender's origin email address.
